Privacy Policy (GDPR)

Last updated: 2026-03-26

1. Controller and scope

This app processes personal data only for user authentication and app functionality.

2. Data processed

Authentication providers may share your email, display name, and unique account ID. The app also stores your local preferences and menu data in your browser local storage.

3. Legal basis

Processing is based on your explicit consent and performance of requested account-access functionality.

4. Purpose and minimization

Data is used only for login, account management, and saving planner settings. No marketing profiling is performed.

5. Data retention

Local data remains in your browser until you clear it or use account/data deletion actions. Authentication data is managed by the selected provider.

6. Your GDPR rights

You can access/export your app data and request deletion through the in-app account panel.

7. Third-party processors

Authentication is provided through Firebase Authentication and selected identity providers (Google, Facebook, X).

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner/maintainer of this deployment.
